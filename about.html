About Sankalp Hospital

Bringing world-class healthcare to Ambikapur and surrounding regions since 2008

Our Vision

To bring world-class healthcare facilities for the people of Surguja and its neighbouring areas in Chhattisgarh state. To adapt a single standard of care for all rich and poor alike. To spread knowledge and the concept of personalized care to distant parts of this country.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to every individual who walks through our doors. We are committed to clinical excellence, patient safety, and compassionate care. Our mission is to make super-specialty medical services available in rural Chhattisgarh.

Our Core Values

  • CompassionTreating every patient with empathy and dignity
  • ExcellenceStriving for the highest standards in clinical care
  • IntegrityHonest and transparent healthcare practices
  • AccessibilityHealthcare available to all, regardless of background

Dr. Sanjay Goyal

Founder & Director, Sankalp Hospital

"Healthcare is not a privilege for the few but a fundamental right of every individual. At Sankalp Hospital, we have worked tirelessly to bring the best medical facilities to Ambikapur and surrounding regions. Our journey from a modest nursing home to a multi-specialty super-care hospital reflects our commitment to excellence and our passion for serving the community."

Over the years, we have introduced advanced technologies, recruited highly skilled specialists, and created an environment where patients feel safe and cared for. Our team believes in the philosophy that every patient deserves the best possible care, and we continue to strive for clinical excellence every single day.
