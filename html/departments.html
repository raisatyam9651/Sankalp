Department Details

Ophthalmology Department

Our Ophthalmology department is equipped with the latest diagnostic and surgical equipment to provide comprehensive eye care. We offer services from routine eye checkups to complex surgeries including cataract extraction with premium IOL implants, LASIK for vision correction, treatment for diabetic retinopathy, and glaucoma management.

  • Cataract Surgery
  • LASIK
  • Retina Services
  • Glaucoma Treatment
  • Squint Correction
  • Eye Injections

Obstetrics & Gynecology

The Obstetrics & Gynecology department provides comprehensive women's health services including prenatal care, normal and cesarean deliveries, high-risk pregnancy management, infertility treatments, and gynecological surgeries. Our team of experienced gynecologists ensures the best care for women at every stage of life.

  • Normal Delivery
  • C-Section
  • Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Fertility Treatment
  • High-Risk Pregnancy
  • Menopause Care

Emergency & Trauma Services

Our 24/7 Emergency and Trauma Centre is equipped to handle all kinds of medical emergencies. With a dedicated team of emergency physicians, modern life-support equipment, and fully equipped ambulances, we ensure immediate medical attention for critical patients.

  • 24/7 Availability
  • Trauma Care
  • Cardiac Emergencies
  • Road Accident Care
  • Poisoning Management
  • ICU Backup
