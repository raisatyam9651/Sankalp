Our Fertility Treatments

IVF Treatment Process

Our step-by-step approach ensures you understand every stage of your fertility journey.

1

Initial Consultation

Complete evaluation of both partners including medical history, physical examination, and necessary tests.

2

Ovarian Stimulation

Medications to stimulate multiple egg production. Regular monitoring through ultrasound and blood tests.

3

Egg Retrieval

Minor procedure under sedation to collect eggs. Takes about 15-20 minutes with minimal discomfort.

4

Fertilization & Culture

Eggs are combined with sperm in our laboratory. Embryos are cultured for 3-5 days for optimal development.

5

Embryo Transfer

The best quality embryo(s) are transferred into the uterus. Simple, painless procedure like a pap smear.

6

Pregnancy Test

Blood test (Beta hCG) after 14 days to confirm pregnancy. Follow-up scans to monitor progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the success rate of IVF?

Our IVF success rate is 60-70% per cycle. Success depends on various factors including age, cause of infertility, and embryo quality. We provide detailed consultation to help you understand your specific chances.

How long does one IVF cycle take?

One complete IVF cycle from start to pregnancy test takes approximately 4-6 weeks. The actual treatment period is about 2-3 weeks, but preparation and initial consultations may take longer.

Is IVF treatment painful?

The injections for ovarian stimulation are given with very fine needles and cause minimal discomfort. Egg retrieval is done under sedation so you won't feel pain. Embryo transfer is a simple, painless procedure similar to a pap smear.

What is the cost of IVF treatment?

IVF costs start from ₹1,50,000 and IUI from ₹25,000. The exact cost depends on the specific treatment required. We offer competitive packages and flexible payment options. Consultation is free.

When should we consider IVF?

You should consider IVF if you've been trying to conceive for over a year without success, have blocked fallopian tubes, severe male factor infertility, endometriosis, or have failed other fertility treatments.

Are there any risks with IVF?

IVF is generally safe. Minor risks include OHSS (Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome) which is carefully monitored and managed. Multiple pregnancy can be avoided by limiting embryo transfers. Our team ensures your safety throughout.
