Sankalp Hospital - Best Hospital In Ambikapur, Chhattisgarh

Sankalp Hospital began its journey in December, 2008 with the vision that was seen many years ago by our founders. One that would bring world class healthcare facilities for the people of Surguja and its neighbouring areas in Chhattisgarh state; Sankalp Hospital Ambikapur has super-care hospital which is the most recommend Best Hospital in Ambikapur One that would adapt a single standard of care for all rich and poor alike; One that would spread knowledge and the concept of personalized care to distant parts of this country.

In the past 11 years, Sankalp hospital has gained excellence and is acclaimed for its quality care and compassion. Sankalp Hospital today has grown from an eye & gynaecology nursing home to a multi-specialty hospital with advanced technology and state of the art facilities. Sankalp hospital Best Hospital In Chhattisgarh.
